Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Easy Egg Fried Rice recipe which nicely uses up leftover rice from the night before. This dish is ready before you know it and is an excellent lunchtime meal or side dish for that Friday night bonanza.

Ingredients list

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 spring onions
  • 250g day old rice
  • 100g frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 block frozen ginger paste
  • 1 block frozen garlic paste
  • Light & dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Gouchujang
  • 1 tsp Seasame Oil
  • 1 tsp MSG
  • Siracha to taste

Method:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok and add eggs. Scramble until golden.
  2. Whilst the eggs scramble, chop the spring onion and add the white part to the egg once ready and cook for 1 min.
  3. Add in the day old rice and frozen vegetables at this point alongside the frozen garlic and ginger paste and cook through for 4 mins. (We use day old rice so that the rice doesn't break apart and gives a much nicer texture when completed.)
  4. Then add the Gouchujang, light soy sauce and teaspoon of MSG, mixing together as each ingredient is added. Then add the seasame oil and allow to cook for 2 mins.
  5. Add Siricha to taste and garnish with the green parts of the spring onion and some seasame seeds if you have them.
