Lasagna Recipe

Classic homemade Lasagna using simple ingredients, perfet for a comforting family meal of four. Ready in just 45 minutes.

Ingredients list

  • 2 tbsp of oil
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g prosciutto
  • Jar of Tomato Sauce
  • 200ml beef stock
  • Pinch of grated nutmeg
  • 300g of fresh Lasagna Sheets
  • Jar of Bechamel Sauce
  • 125g fresh mozzarella, torn into chunks

  1. Make meat suace by heating 2 tbsp of oil
