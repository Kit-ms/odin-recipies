Lasagna Recipe

Classic homemade Lasagna using simple ingredients, perfet for a comforting family meal of four. Ready in just 45 minutes.

Ingredients list

  • 2 tbsp of oil
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g prosciutto
  • Jar of Tomato Sauce
  • 200ml beef stock
  • Pinch of grated nutmeg
  • 300g of fresh Lasagna Sheets
  • Jar of Bechamel Sauce
  • 125g fresh mozzarella, torn into chunks

Method:

  1. Pre heat oven to 180C/fan/160C/gas 4 and lightly grease an overproof dish approx. 30 x 20cm
  2. Make meat suace by heating 2 tbsp of oil in a large frying pan and cook the beef mince for 10 mins until browned.
  3. Finely chop 4 slices of prosciutto and then stir inot the meat mixture.
  4. Pour over 800g of the tomato sauce and add the beef stock to the pan. Season with salt and pepper, plus the nutmeg.
  5. Bring the pan to the boil and simmer for 30 mins until the sauce has thickened and looks rich.
  6. Spoon one thrid of the meat sauce into the dish, then layer with the lasagna sheets. Add 130g of the ready-made Bechamel Sauce.
  7. Repeat this process until there are three layers of pasta. Using the rest of the Bechamel sauce, cover the pasta so that there are no bits of pasta poking out.
  8. Scatter the torn mozzarella pieces over the top of the lasagna.
  9. Arrange the remaining prosciutto on top of the dish and place into the oven for 45 minutes, or until the top is bubbling and lightly brwoned.
