Crispy Tofu with vegetables Recipe

Simple dish is deliciously tasty and definitely worth the slighty longer time you may be waitng for your dinner. The tofu can easily be switched out for any other protein source and so can the vegetables for anything you may have in the fridge.

Ingredients list

For the Tofu:
  • 100 ml vegetable oil
  • 450g Firm Tofu
  • 100g Corn flour
  • 1 tsp Sashimi
  • 1 tsp MSG
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 egg
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
For the Sauce:
  • 3 tbps honey
  • Light & Dark Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Chinese Vinegar
  • 1 tsp MSG
  • 1 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 1 tsp Seasame seeds
  • Siricha to taste
  • Seasame Oil

Method:

  1. In a bowl combine the corn flour and the dry seasonings.
  2. In a seperate bowl, crack the egg and whisk together to make an egg wash.
  3. Into another bowl place the panko breadcrumbs so that there are three seperate bowls ready for dipping.
